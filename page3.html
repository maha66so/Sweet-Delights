Simple Cookies Recipe

Ingredients Amount
brown sugar 1 ½ cups
softened butter 1 cup
eggs 2
all-purpose flour 3 cups
chocolate chips 1 ½ cups
  1. Gather the ingredients. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Stir sugar, flour, cocoa, baking powder, baking soda, and salt together in a large bowl.
  3. Beat sugar and butter together in a large bowl with an electric mixer until light and fluffier in texture.
    Beat in eggs, one at at time. Gradually stir in flour until well combined; fold in chocolate chips.
  4. Spoon dough onto a baking sheet, keeping them about 2 inches apart.
    Bake in the preheated oven until edges are golden brown, 10 to 15 minutes.
  5. Ready to serve!
